Process

From topic to presentation
in four steps

No design skills or copywriting experience needed. The AI does all of it.

1

Describe your topic

Type your subject in plain language — "Q3 investor update", "Introduction to machine learning", "Wedding planning timeline" — anything works. No special formatting required.

2

Set your preferences

The AI chatbot asks for your audience, tone, number of slides, and any specific points you want covered. You can be as brief or as detailed as you like.

3

AI generates your slides

Watch in real time as the AI writes structured headlines, concise bullet points, and speaker-note-ready content for every slide — optimized for clarity and impact.

4

Export & present

Download your presentation as an editable PPTX or a publication-ready PDF. Wide layout for screens and projectors; vertical layout for mobile and sharing apps.
